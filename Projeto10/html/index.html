Aigis

Aigis is a key character in Persona 3, an anti-Shadow android with a complex personality and deep emotional growth throughout the game. Designed by the Kirijo Group as part of the Anti-Shadow Suppression Weapon project, she possesses advanced combat capabilities, including powerful weaponry integrated into her body. Despite being a machine, Aigis develops human-like emotions over time, making her one of the most compelling characters in the game.
