One Piece

The One Piece Wano arc is one of the series' most action-packed and visually stunning storylines. It follows the Straw Hat Pirates and their allies as they unite to overthrow Kaido and free Wano from his tyrannical rule. The arc features intense battles, significant character growth—especially for Luffy and Zoro—and breathtaking animation.
